Hotdog

A hot dog is a food consisting of a grilled or steamed sausage served in the slit of a partially
bun. The term hot dog can also refer to the sausage itself. The sausage used is a wiener or a frankfurter.

The names of these sausages also commonly refer to their assembled dish.

Ingredients

  1. hotdog
  2. mustard
  3. ketchup
  4. bun

Steps:

  • grill the hotdog.
  • add hotdog to the bun.
  • add mustard.
  • add ketchup.
  • enjoy.
